Introduction

This page is meant to serve as an outline of a super app meant to help someone travel.

The challenge of a travel super app

Travelling around the world can be a difficult task. There are many customs, rules and regulations that a user may not be aware of. This app is meant to help ease the process by providing information about the place you are travelling to, as well as, providing a single app that allows you to book, research, rent and prepare for any journey ahead of you.

Research Methods

A survey was conducted to find the 'pain-points' of common travel apps. It was found that users value an app that:

  • Keeps information all in one place
  • Notifies the user if there are any changes to their vacation (via SMS or a notification)
  • Responsive, intuitive and fluid experience
